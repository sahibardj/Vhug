Regular Pads are widely accepted as they are comfortable to use. Women find ease in managing their menstrual cycle with them. They can be safely used for 6-7 hours. They may have a cotton top or porous plastic top; they hold blood by absorbing it. Available in various sizes, we can choose one based on menstrual flow.

Pads offer plenty of benefits to users, boasting a safe and effective way to deal with your body's changing needs. They're also simple to change - and more comfortable to determine when changing is necessary - leading to leak-proof protection on even your heaviest days. Pads also offer plenty of diversity in use; with so many styles, there's a perfect fit for any occasion.




PROS CONS
Easy to use. May lead to rashes as moisture is trapped in for a long time.
The need for a change can be easily determined. You may experience a problem with a foul smell.
If appropriately placed eradicates chances of leakage. It's not an eco-friendly option.

Biodegradable pads are an effort to reduce ever-increasing waste generation by humankind. The most significant benefit of anything biodegradable is that we can compost it in our backyard; otherwise, it must be sent to landfills (involving waste management workers, systems and elaborate processes). Most of these 'biodegradable' pads claim to be chemical-free and compostable.

Many of these claim to be made of organic cotton top sheet along with bamboo pulp for extra leakage protection. This is individually wrapped in disposable bags. They take much less time to decompose since they are made of sustainable ingredients like bamboo pulp and organic cotton. These materials keep our planet safe.


Reusable pads are usually made up of several layers of cotton or hemp. They are the same as regular pads, minus the plastic. They are an eco and budget-friendly replacement for regular pads. One pad can be used several times and can be disposed of easily when worn out. These pads can be safely used for about 5-6 hours, depending on flow. After each use, they need to be washed clean and should be left with no stain to avoid any form of infection. Most pads come with a thin lining preventing them from staining.




PROS CONS
Comfort: They are incredibly comfortable and feel soft down there. Not suitable for heavy flow.
No Rash-There are no chances of your skin developing rashes since cloths do not block your sweat or any form of moisture. Sometimes it can be a little tricky to wash the bloodstains away.
No foul smell as no plastic is reacting with blood.
It will last much longer, and thus you save money. 		May create problems while traveling as washing them might be a challenging task.
